Design - While we were designing our boat we found many different types to use as examples. We founs boats with higher sides will prevent water from seeping through the top. We made our boat short to conserve the tap and make sure water wont leak through sides or holes. Using pointed edges on both sides of a boat is the most common type. The pointed edges allow for the bot to cut through the water. It will allow us a smoother sail. Another common thing we saw was rounded bottoms but we thought it was be a challenge to be able to float. So we decided to stick with a flat bottom.

Building proccess- When we started building our boat we started with measurments to make sure the cardboard seams fit perfectly so we dont have leaking. We tried our best to tape in only necessary areas since we are limited to one roll of tape. Making sure our cardboard was cut evenly is very important and if it wasnt cut evenly we went back to either fix it or re-make it. After we had all the sides and base set we started taping them toghether. We tapes both inside the boat and outside to make sure there was no water leaking. Overall at the end we still had a little less than half our roll of tape left over which we could of used to re-tape our boat more.
